# Product Guide: HomeLab Connect

## Initial Concept
A comprehensive SaaS marketplace for booking at-home diagnostic lab tests, connecting patients with partner labs for home sample collection and digital report delivery. Inspired by AmarLab and similar platforms, it aims to provide convenient, transparent, and affordable healthcare access.

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Individual patients and families seeking routine checkups, chronic disease monitoring, and preventive care.
- **Secondary:**
    - **Healthcare Providers:** Doctors, clinics, and labs managing appointments and patient data.
    - **Corporate Clients:** Organizations requiring employee health checkups and wellness tracking.
    - **Caregivers:** Individuals managing health needs for elderly parents or family members.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Convenience:** Home sample collection saves time and reduces exposure risks.
- **Transparency:** Compare prices, turnaround times, and ratings across multiple partner labs.
- **Accessibility:** Digital booking and report delivery make diagnostics accessible to elderly, disabled, and busy individuals.
- **Comprehensive Care:** Wide range of tests and packages (500+ tests, 50+ packages) catered to various health needs.

## Key Features
- **User Platform (Web & Mobile):**
    - **Test Discovery:** Search by test name, category, symptoms, or packages.
    - **Lab Selection:** Choose labs based on rating, price, and accreditation.
    - **Booking Flow:** Cart system, family profiles, address management, and slot selection.
    - **Order Management:** Real-time tracking from booking to report delivery.
    - **Digital Reports:** Secure storage, trend analysis, and PDF downloads.
- **Partner & Operations:**
    - **Lab Dashboard:** Order management, report upload, and analytics.
    - **Phlebotomist App:** Assignment management, navigation, and sample tracking.
    - **Admin Dashboard:** Comprehensive control over catalog, users, orders, and logistics.

## Monetization Strategy
- **Commission:** Percentage fee on bookings from partner labs.
- **Service Fees:** Fixed convenience fee for home collection.
- **Premium Services:** Subscriptions, priority slots, and hardcopy reports.
- **B2B Partnerships:** Corporate wellness packages.

## Success Metrics (MVP)
- **Acquisition:** 1,000+ user sign-ups in Q1.
- **Conversion:** >20% booking conversion rate.
- **Retention:** >15% repeat bookings.
- **Satisfaction:** NPS >40.
- **Operations:** >90% successful end-to-end order completion.
